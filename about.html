About me

My name is Mats and I am originally from a small town called Nora outside Örebro where I currently live. I am passionate about ensuring that software is of the highest quality through rigorous testing and have an eye for detail and a strong drive to contribute to high-quality solutions.

Nora stad

I have two years of experience in software testing and have worked with both manual and automated tests to identify and prevent bugs. I have experience with testing tools such as Playwright and Selenium as well as test management in Jira.

In addition, I have basic programming skills (Java, C#, Javascript, etc.), which helps me write and analyze automated tests. Working in an Agile team is something I enjoy, as it provides the opportunity for close collaboration and continuous improvement of the testing processes. I am currently taking a course in Fullstack to further broaden my knowledge.

As a person, I am meticulous, solution-oriented and have a high work ethic. I appreciate challenges and thrive in an environment where I can use my analytical skills to identify and solve problems. My colleagues usually describe me as a committed and collaborative person who always strives to deliver high quality.

So if you are interested in working with a driven and committed person, just get in touch. Click on "Contact me" and we will be in touch!

Kind regards Mats
